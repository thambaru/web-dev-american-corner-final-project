About Us
 

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for people to share their recipes with the world. We believe that everyone has a unique recipe that they can share with others. We want to make it easy for people to share their recipes with others and to find new recipes to try.

About the web template

CookBook is a recipe website template created by WD101 for the partial fulfillment of the course Web Design & Development conducted by Ms. Tharushini Sabeendra at American Corner, Matara, Sri Lanka.
