Pol Sambol

Pol Sambol is a delicious Sri Lankan condiment made with a blend of spicy flavors. Its combination of onions, coconut, and tangy lemon juice perfectly complements dishes like curry and rice. This homemade dish is a must-try for those who enjoy a burst of flavors in their meals.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup desiccated coconut
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup red chilli, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup salt

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Enjoy!
